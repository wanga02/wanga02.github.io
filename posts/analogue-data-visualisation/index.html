Analogue data visualisation (ft. Dear Data)

2021-01-10
analogue visualisation
art

In 2016 I went to the Big Bang Data exhibit at Somerset House and fell hard and fast for Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec’s art project. My good friend Maria (of Studio HOTmess) and I decided to take on the challenge. The idea is to record and visualise data around one aspect of your life each week, and then send it to your partner in crime in the post.

We reprised the habit for a couple of months during the first 2020 lockdown.

My designs below. I’ve also included an extra piece of analogue data viz that I did in a visualisation workshop - a temporary tattoo designed from personal data about coffee drinking, or a ‘stattoo’!
